## Concept

Terraviza turns climate-risk intelligence into asset-level decisions for real estate owners, insurers, lenders and municipalities.

The mark uses three property blocks to represent asset portfolios, climate exposure and decision layers. The orange curve acts as a climate-finance signal: risk moving through budgets, insurance pressure and adaptation planning. The warmer off-white palette avoids the repeated black SaaS look and gives Terraviza a more premium real-estate and climate-intelligence feel.
